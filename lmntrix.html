Breach Intelligence Search

This was a core investigation feature for exploring data breaches and exposed employee sessions. The platform aggregated large-scale breach data, including compromised sessions, cookies, and related metadata. Analysts needed to investigate exposure events, but the data was fragmented and required switching between filters and tools to narrow down cases.

I designed a unified search experience with multiple parameters — domain, country, keywords, and combined filters. I worked on filter interactions, result structure, and how to keep complex data readable without overwhelming users. The flow became more flexible: analysts could start from one entry point and refine searches instead of rebuilding them across tools.
